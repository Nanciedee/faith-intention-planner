# faith-intention-planner
A 2026 Planner for reflexion on life
The Faith & Intention Planner is an 8.5x11" digital planner designed for Etsy and personal use. It combines elegant design with practical tracking features for spiritual growth, creative pursuits, health goals, and emotional wellness.

- **Cover Page**: Elegant watercolor design with faith-centered branding
- **December 2025 Intro**: Reflection and transition page for the new year
- **Q1 2026 Focus Page**: Quarterly planning with monthly breakdowns
- **Monthly Focus Trackers**: Track Creative, Health, Family, and Spiritual goals
- **Weekly Rituals Pages**: Daily spiritual, creative, fitness, and reflection tracking
- **Daily Micro-Trackers**: Energy, Movement, Prayer, and Connection tracking
- **Emotional Success Section**: Gratitude, affirmations, and mood tracking
